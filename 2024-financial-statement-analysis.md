Risk is not just a thing to avoid; it is a way to understand what a decision depends on

When I think about risk, I try to separate three questions: what has to be true, what could go wrong, and what would change my mind

## From Fear To Structure

The most useful risk work is not dramatic; it is patient, specific, and connected to assumptions

In finance, a model can look precise while still hiding fragile inputs; the discipline is to notice which inputs matter most and where confidence should be limited

## The Habit I Want To Build

I want risk-aware thinking to become a daily operating habit: name the range, check the downside, and keep the decision honest
