Residual income valuation starts with book value and adds the present value of income earned above the required return on equity

## Core Formula

Residual income is net income minus an equity charge

The equity charge equals beginning book value multiplied by the required return on equity

## Why It Matters

The model is useful when dividends or free cash flow are less stable, but accounting quality and clean surplus assumptions need careful attention
