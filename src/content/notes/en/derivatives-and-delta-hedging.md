Delta measures how much an option price is expected to change when the underlying asset changes by one unit

## Hedging Logic

A delta hedge tries to offset directional exposure by taking a position in the underlying asset

The hedge is not permanent because delta changes as price, time, and volatility change

## Risk Reminder

Delta hedging reduces one type of exposure, but it does not eliminate gamma, volatility, liquidity, or model risk
