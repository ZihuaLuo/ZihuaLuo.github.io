This note is a lightweight map for organizing CFA Level II study

## Core Buckets

- Financial Statement Analysis: quality of earnings, intercorporate investments, pensions, multinational operations
- Equity Valuation: multiples, discounted cash flow, residual income, private company valuation
- Fixed Income: term structure, credit analysis, valuation under changing rate assumptions
- Derivatives: forwards, futures, swaps, options, and how payoff structures translate into risk
- Portfolio Management: factor exposure, active risk, performance attribution, and constraints

## Study Principle

The main goal is not to memorize each reading in isolation; the more useful goal is to connect concepts by asking: what assumption is changing, what risk is being measured, and what decision would this analysis support?
