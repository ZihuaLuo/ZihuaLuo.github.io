Research is not the same as collecting information

The discipline begins when information is tested against context, compared with alternatives, and interpreted with care

## Evidence Needs Framing

Data rarely speaks cleanly on its own; it needs a question, a boundary, and an understanding of where it came from

That is why research and analytics belong together: one gives evidence structure, the other keeps interpretation accountable

## The Standard I Am Practicing

I want my research notes to become more than summaries; they should show how a conclusion was reached and what uncertainty remains
