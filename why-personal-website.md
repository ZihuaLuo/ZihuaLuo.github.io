Quality of earnings asks whether reported profit is a reliable signal of economic performance

## Checklist

- Revenue: Is growth supported by cash collection, or mostly by receivables?
- Margins: Are margin improvements operational, accounting-driven, or temporary?
- Accruals: Are accruals rising faster than earnings and cash flow?
- One-time items: Are unusual gains or restructuring charges distorting comparability?
- Cash flow: Does operating cash flow confirm the earnings story?

## Working Question

If the accounting presentation changed tomorrow, would the underlying business story still look as strong?
