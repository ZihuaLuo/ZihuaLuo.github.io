The yield curve is a compact view of how the market prices money across time

## What To Watch

- Level: the broad rate environment
- Slope: the relationship between short and long maturities
- Curvature: where changes are concentrated across the curve
- Spreads: compensation for credit, liquidity, and other risks

## Working Question

What does the curve imply about growth, inflation, policy, and the compensation required for holding duration?
