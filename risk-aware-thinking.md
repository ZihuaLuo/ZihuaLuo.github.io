Financial statement analysis is most useful when the statements are read as one connected system

## Framework

- Income statement: growth, margins, operating leverage
- Balance sheet: capital intensity, working capital, financing structure
- Cash flow: conversion quality, reinvestment needs, durability
- Notes: accounting choices, assumptions, and comparability limits

## Working Question

What does the accounting record reveal about the economic engine of the business?
